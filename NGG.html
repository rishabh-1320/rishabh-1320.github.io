When it comes to sport's Olympic games is worlds largest athletics festival.
What about India's biggest athletics festival?

That is National Games, held once every four years
OVERVIEW

Last National games was held in Kerala 2015, next was supposed to happen in 2019, but due to delay's & corona it was postponed.
Hence, "National Games Gujarat 2022"

Me working in a team of 3 completed the website from start to finish. from planning to complete final project.

graphic designers and content writers, helped us a lot.

From the begging

To jump start the project we took reference from last national games site Kerala 2015.

The goal was to prepare a site map, which will act as a compass as we move forward with the project.
