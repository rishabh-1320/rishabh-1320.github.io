A job platform for Niftians and design students to help them in their careers by integrating alumni support.
OVERVIEW

When out of college and looking for jobs, it can be little intimidating. As there are lot of things, tough competition, lack of experience, and what not.

There are many services and job portals too.


While I myself have faced this, so I wanted to create a platform. A job where students can be in direct touch with their alumni. (the initial Idea was only for Niftians).

Hope you like it.

Discover

Interviews were conducted to better understand the user.
NIFT alumni were decided as the perfect candidates to conduct user interviews as they had already gone through the process of getting a job and changing them.

Define

After talking to users, their pain points and other insights were gathered
